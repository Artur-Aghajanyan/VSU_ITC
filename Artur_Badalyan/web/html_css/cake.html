Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely sranulated white suger
Self-raising flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agents (usually salt and baking powder)

Method

  1. Prehent the oven to 190.oC
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. in a medium bowl,whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Baked for 20 minutes in the preheated oven or until the top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed.
